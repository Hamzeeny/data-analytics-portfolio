## 🚴 Bike Sales Dashboard

### Business Problem

A retail business wanted to better understand the characteristics of customers who purchase bicycles. The goal was to identify patterns in customer demographics and purchasing behaviour that could support more effective marketing and sales strategies.

### My Solution

I developed an interactive Excel dashboard that analyzes customer demographics, income levels, education, commute distances, and purchasing behaviour. Users can explore the data using interactive filters to quickly identify trends and compare different customer groups.

### Business Value

The dashboard helps decision-makers identify customer segments with higher purchase potential, making it easier to develop targeted marketing campaigns and improve customer engagement.

**Tools Used:** Microsoft Excel
